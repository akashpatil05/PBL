Our Mission
Helping You Reunite
The Lost and Found Hub is a user-friendly website that helps individuals, specifically students, locate lost items or report found items. Users can post details about their lost items and search for items found by others. The platform aims to provide a centralized solution for reuniting people with their belongings. The website features buttons for lost and found items, making it simple and user-friendly. The website is launching today with a focus on addressing the immediate needs of the college community. -->
